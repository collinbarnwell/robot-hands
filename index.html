Hello there!

Directions:

  1. The arrow keys move the camera parallel to the ground plane.
  2. 'W' and 'S' move the camera up and down.
  3. 'A' and 'D' rotate the camera direction about the vertical axis.
  4. 'Q' and 'E' rotate the camera about the horizontal axis.
  5. 'I', 'J', 'K' and 'L' move a light source that is suspended above the scene in the cardinal directions, parallel to the ground plane.
  6. Hit 'O' to show/hide the grid lines.
  7. Hit 'F1' to hide or display these instructions (or 'H', or '1').
  8. Enjoy!
